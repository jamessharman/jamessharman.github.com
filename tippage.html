Tips:

  • When you press start, you will be taken to screen with six pages.
  • Each page contains an image and clues to a separate page with an mp3 file on it, which can be downloaded by right clicking and selecting "save as" or "save target as". Finding the link is the trick.
  • You may need to manually enter the link in the address bar once you have found it.
  • If the answer is to enter a url, it will always end with ".html".
  • The clues maybe reasonably straight forward, or relatively vague.
  • The clue might be hidden as well.
  • There may be more than one clue.
  • The image may be there to help or distract you.
  • When you want to put together the mp3 files you have, use Audacity. It is a free piece of audio editing software. All you have to do is drag each mp3 file into Audacity and press play. It can be found here.
